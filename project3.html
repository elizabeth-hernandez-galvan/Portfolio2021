Project 3: Zine—Personal Identity


For this project, the task was to create an 8-page zine about historical or familial experiences, political views, a topic that you’re passionate about, or something that inspires you. Specifications: Zine must be created only in Adobe Photoshop and must use a minimum of 3 images. Zine can be in color or black & white.
