Project 2: Typeface Poster


For this project, the task was to pick a typeface and create a poster that communicates the essential characteristics of letter forms unique to that typeface.
Restrictions: can only use type and color, but type must be the only graphic element in the poster. The poster itself should reflect the nature of the typeface
as currently used and as intended by the original typeface designer. Tools: Illustrator; Typeface: Shelley Script; Duration: 2 weeks
